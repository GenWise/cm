## ğŸ“ Video Format Cheat Sheet

### Master Video (YouTube/Website)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (1080p)
- **Duration**: Any length
- **Use For**: YouTube, website embedding, reference

### Square Version (LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1
- **Resolution**: 1080x1080
- **Duration**: 60-90 seconds ideal
- **Why**: Works well in feeds, higher engagement

### Vertical Portrait (Instagram Feed)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:5
- **Resolution**: 1080x1350
- **Duration**: Under 60 seconds
- **Why**: Takes up more screen space on mobile

### Vertical Full Screen (Reels, TikTok)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920
- **Duration**: 15-60 seconds ideal
- **Why**: Immersive full-screen mobile experience

### WhatsApp Status
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16
- **Resolution**: 720p minimum
- **Duration**: Under 30 seconds
- **File Size**: Under 16MB
- **Why**: Quick sharing, file size limits

## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### Always Add Captions
- 85% of social videos are watched without sound
- Increases watch time by 12%
- Better accessibility
- Helps with algorithm (Facebook reads captions)

### Hook in 3 Seconds
- Viewers decide instantly whether to keep watching
- Start with the most interesting part
- Use pattern interrupts (movement, text, questions)
- Don't waste time on long intros

### Optimal Video Length by Platform
- **LinkedIn**: 30-90 seconds (sweet spot: 60s)
- **Facebook**: 30-60 seconds
- **Instagram Feed**: 30-60 seconds
- **Instagram Reels**: 15-30 seconds (max engagement)
- **TikTok**: 15-60 seconds
- **Twitter**: 30-45 seconds
- **YouTube**: 8-15 minutes (long-form)

### Mobile-First Design
- 75% of social video views are on mobile
- Keep text large and readable
- Use vertical formats when possible
- Test on phone before posting

### Batch Production
- Create all versions at once
- Use export presets in your editor
- Save time with templates
- Maintain consistency
